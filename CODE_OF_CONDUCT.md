# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want a respectful, collaborative, and safe technical community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Respectful and professional communication.
- Criticism focused on code, not people.
- Openness to feedback and learning.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information about others.
- Malicious or destructive behavior against project infrastructure.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, or block contributions in cases of serious or repeated violations.
